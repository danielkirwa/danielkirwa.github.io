Frequently Asked Questions

The required documents vary depending on the visa type and destination country. Generally, applicants may need

  1. A valid passport (with at least 6 months validity)
  2. Completed visa application form
  3. Passport-size photographs
  4. Proof of financial support (bank statements, sponsorship letters, etc.)
  5. Academic transcripts (for student visas)
  6. Employment letter or business registration (for work/business visas)
  7. Invitation letters or travel itinerary (for tourist/business visas)
  8. National ID and supporting documents as required by the embassy

Our team will provide a customized checklist based on your specific visa category and destination.

Processing times vary depending on the country and type of visa. On average:

  1. Student visas: 4–12 weeks
  2. Tourist/Business visas: 2–6 weeks
  3. Residence permits: 2–6 months

We advise clients to begin the application process early to avoid delays. Our consultants will guide you on the best timelines based on your travel goals.

  2. We strengthen your documents and reapply.
  3. We provide appeal support if applicable.
