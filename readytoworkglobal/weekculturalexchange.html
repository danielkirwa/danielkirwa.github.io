🌍 7-Day Cultural Exchange Experience in Ghana

Discover the warmth, heritage, and vibrant culture of Ghana
with Ready To Work Global Consult Limited.

📅 Day 1 – Arrival in Accra

  • Airport pickup and VIP reception
  • Hotel check-in & welcome dinner
  • Evening cultural orientation: Introduction to Ghanaian traditions, music & dance

👉 Highlight: Traditional drumming and dance performance

📅 Day 2 – Accra City & Cultural Tour

  • Visit the Kwame Nkrumah Memorial Park
  • Explore Independence Square & Black Star Gate
  • Walk through Makola Market (local crafts & textiles)
  • Lunch at a traditional Ghanaian restaurant
  • Evening leisure at Labadi Beach

👉 Highlight: Local street food tasting

📅 Day 3 – Cape Coast Heritage Tour

  • Travel to Cape Coast (Central Region)
  • Guided tour of Cape Coast Castle & Elmina Castle (UNESCO World Heritage sites)
  • Walk through Kakum National Park’s Canopy Walkway
  • Dinner by the oceanfront

👉 Highlight: Storytelling session on Ghana’s history and diaspora connections

📅 Day 4 – Cultural Immersion & Community Visit

  • Visit a rural community for traditional cooking lessons
  • Learn to prepare local dishes (fufu, banku, or jollof rice)
  • Engage with artisans: kente weaving, bead making, and pottery
  • Evening cultural exchange discussion with locals

👉 Highlight: Hands-on cultural workshops

📅 Day 5 – Ashanti Kingdom Experience (Kumasi)

  • Travel to Kumasi – the Ashanti Region
  • Visit the Manhyia Palace Museum (home of the Ashanti King)
  • Tour the Kejetia Market – one of West Africa’s largest markets
  • Evening storytelling & drumming session

👉 Highlight: Insight into Ashanti culture, royalty, and heritage

📅 Day 6 – Nature & Adventure Day

  • Visit Lake Bosomtwe (Ghana’s only natural lake)
  • Canoeing and eco-tourism activities
  • Relaxation and reflection session by the lake
  • Farewell cultural dinner with live band & dance

👉 Highlight: Insight into Ashanti culture, royalty, and heritage
