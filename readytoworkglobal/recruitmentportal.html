🌍 Recruitment Portal

At Ready To Work Global Consult Limited, our recruitment portal is designed to create a smooth, transparent, and efficient link between job seekers and employers. Whether you are looking for your next career opportunity or searching for the right talent to grow your company, our portal provides a trusted platform to connect.

👩‍🎓 For Applicants – Start Your Career Journey

We help qualified graduates and professionals find the right opportunities both in Ghana and abroad.

  • ✅ Submit Your Details: Fill in the application form and upload your CV, certificates, and identification documents.
  • ✅ Verification & Screening: Our team will verify your documents and conduct a pre-interview online session to assess your competence.
  • ✅ Get Matched: Once approved, your profile is added to our database of qualified candidates.
  • ✅ Opportunities Await:We connect you with partner companies locally and internationally that best match your skills.

What You Need to Apply

  • ✅ Updated CV/Resume
  • ✅ Academic Certificates & Transcripts
  • ✅ Identification (Ghana Card / Passport)
  • ✅ Professional Certifications (if any)

📌 Note: Only applicants who pass our verification and competence assessment will be shortlisted for job referrals.

🏢 For Employers – Hire the Right Talent

Finding the right candidate doesn’t have to be stressful. We make it easy for companies to access a pre-screened pool of competent graduates and professionals.

How We Support Employers

  • Access to our candidate database of qualified and verified applicants.
  • Customized candidate matching to fit your job role and organizational culture.
  • Streamlined shortlisting process with only the most competent applicants forwarded.
  • Ongoing support to ensure smooth integration of new hires into your workforce.

How to Get Started

  • Submit your hiring request through our employer form.
  • Indicate job title, requirements, and specific qualifications needed.
  • Receive a shortlist of pre-screened candidates.
  • Select and hire with confidence.

⚖️ Our Commitment

  • For Applicants: A fair, transparent, and professional recruitment process that helps you achieve your career goals.
  • For Employers: A reliable, stress-free hiring process that ensures you get the best talent every time.

At Ready To Work Global Consult Limited, we are not just recruiters—we are career builders and business growth partners.
