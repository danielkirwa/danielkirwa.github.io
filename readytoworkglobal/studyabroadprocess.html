🎓 STUDY ABROAD PROCESS

We make the journey simple, clear, and stress-free. Here’s how it works:

Step 1: Pre-Screening Engagement 🔍

Meet with our educational advisors for an initial assessment.

  • Review of academic background & qualifications.
  • Identify suitable study destinations & opportunities.

Step 2: Document Submission 📑

Provide the necessary documents for verification:

  • Academic Certificates & Transcripts
  • Passport & National ID
  • CV/Resume and other relevant information

Step 3: Admission Application 🏫

We submit your application to our trusted partner institutions abroad.

  • Guidance on program selection
  • Professional application support

Step 4: Funding Opportunities 💰

We help secure financial support:

  • Full & Partial Scholarships
  • Graduate Assistantships
  • Exclusive funding packages from partner schools

Step 5: Visa Application & Approval 🛂

End-to-end support to ensure visa success:

  • Preparation of embassy-standard documents
  • Application submission & interview guidance

Step 6: Departure & Orientation ✈️

Get ready for your new journey:

  • Pre-departure orientation & travel assistance
  • Smooth transition into your new academic life abroad

🌍 With Ready To Work Global Consult Limited, your dream of studying abroad is just six steps away!
